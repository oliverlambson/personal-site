Bio

Programmer and builder of Carbon Fibre guitars. Constantly tinkering with something interesting.

Masters in Manufacturing and Management from Cambridge University, and Bachelor of Mechatronic Engineering from Stellenbosch University.

Founded Rubato Guitars, a boutique carbon fibre electric guitar company. Built lots of useful things at Lendable from 2022-24: part of the core team that launched and scaled the US business.
